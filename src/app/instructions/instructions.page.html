Instructions Getting Started To play Word License, select the "Play" option from the home page and then click the "Start" button. How to Play The concept behind Word License is fairly simple. You are given a license plate with three letters and three numbers. The goal for each set of three letters is to find a word in the dictionary that contains those three letters in the given order. The letters do not have to appear in the word consecutively. Example For example, given the letters 'LEC', possible words include, but are not limited to "leach", "lecture", and "electric". Note that the word does not have to begin with the first letter in the given set of three. Notice Every three letter combination corresponds to at least one word in the dictionary. (even if it doesn't seem like it!)
